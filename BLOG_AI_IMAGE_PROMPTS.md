- **Format**: WebP (preferred) or PNG
- **Color Mode**: RGB
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (under 300KB)

### Style Consistency
- Deep navy blue background (#0A1628 or similar)
- Gold/bronze planetary symbols and zodiac signs
- **Ultra-wide horizontal banner format** (NOT circular wheel)
- Minimalist yet professional appearance
- Clear, centered composition with safe margins
- **15% margin at top and bottom** to prevent cropping

### Critical Design Notes
- **MUST use 4:3 aspect ratio** - verify width is exactly double the height
- **Avoid circular astrology wheels** - they get cropped in 4:3 format
- **Use flat horizontal layout** - zodiac elements on left/right sides only
- **Ensure full visibility** - all elements within safe zone (15% margin top/bottom)
- **Planetary symbol centered** - main focus in the middle
- **World map horizontal** - continents span left to right, very faint
- **Stars in corners only** - avoid cluttering the center
- **NO lines extending to edges** - keep all elements within safe margins

### Symbol Accuracy
- Use accurate Unicode astrological symbols
- Ensure symbols are clearly visible and properly formed
- Maintain consistent symbol sizing and prominence
