## What Are Sun Lines in Astrocartography?

### How Sun Lines Are Calculated

Sun lines in astrocartography mark locations where the Sun was positioned at one of the four angles (Ascendant, Descendant, Imum Coeli, or Midheaven) at the exact moment of your birth. These lines are calculated based on your precise birth date, time, and location.

The calculation projects the Sun's position at your birth moment onto geographical locations around the world. Where the Sun was rising (Ascendant), setting (Descendant), at the lowest point (IC), or highest point (MC) in the sky creates four distinct Sun lines curving across the globe.

Because these lines are based on your exact birth data, your Sun lines are unique to you. Two people born on the same day but at different times or locations will have completely different Sun line placements. This personalization makes astrocartography powerful for understanding where your authentic self shines most brightly.

The lines curve across the map following the mathematical projection of the Sun's position at your birth. Understanding where these lines run helps you identify locations where your confidence, vitality, and authentic expression will be most accessible.

### The Four Sun Lines Explained

Each of the four Sun lines affects different life areas, though all carry the Sun's core themes of confidence, vitality, and authentic expression. Understanding these distinctions helps you choose Sun line locations aligned with your goals.

**Sun Ascendant (AC) Line**: Affects your identity, appearance, and how others perceive you. On this line, you naturally project confidence, vitality, and authentic presence. You feel most like yourself.

**Sun Descendant (DC) Line**: Affects relationships and partnerships. On this line, you attract partners who see and appreciate your authentic self. Relationships support your self-expression and confidence.

**Sun Imum Coeli (IC) Line**: Affects home, family, and emotional foundation. On this line, you feel emotionally secure in your identity and create a home that reflects your authentic self.

**Sun Midheaven (MC) Line**: Affects career, public reputation, and life purpose. This is the most powerful Sun line for recognition, career success, and expressing your purpose publicly.

### Orb of Influence

Sun lines have an orb of influence extending approximately 400-500 miles on either side of the exact line. The closer you are to the line, the more intensely you'll experience the Sun's radiant energy. Even being within this orb can activate Sun themes.

The orb means you don't need to live exactly on the line to benefit from the Sun's energy. Cities within a few hundred miles of your Sun line will still carry significant solar influence, making more locations accessible for experiencing these vitalizing energies.

Crossing points where Sun lines intersect with other planetary lines create particularly powerful locations. For example, a Sun-Jupiter crossing might amplify success and recognition, while a Sun-Venus crossing could enhance creative expression and attractiveness.

When considering relocation or travel, check both the exact Sun line and the broader orb. You might find that a city where you felt most alive and confident sits within your Sun line orb, explaining that experience.
